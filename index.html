Our Story

Redfern's best.

Berry Acai Revolution is Redfern's sanctuary for fresh, delicious and guilt-free goodness. We're proudly family-owned, offer gluten-free options across the menu, and back local Aussie producers wherever we can.

Our bowls are made with at least 60% açaí concentrate — that's 40% more than most conventional açaí stores — so you get real flavour and real nutrition in every serve.
